Our History

In 1992, Mr. Kunal Shah established environmental consultancy firm in Surat. Thereafter, En-vision Group of Companies has been engaged in different environmental activities throughout India. Currently, En-vision has more than 400 clients in different sectors and 180 employees all over India.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to bring integrity, commitment, accountability, innovation, and technical stewardship to meet clients' and society's expectations in the field of environment.

Our Vision

To be a leader in delivering Environmentally sound and Sustainable solutions, that consistently improve the performance of our clients towards Environment and ultimately leading to build a better community.

Our Quality Policy

It is our endeavour to provide technically feasible and economically viable solutions in Environmental Engineering, Monitoring & Analysis, Pollution Control and other Engineering disciplines; which will satisfy customer's need as well as statutory and regulatory requirements.

This shall be achieved by a clear focus on following areas:

  • Commitment for quality services in environment management which meet the standards.
  • Recruiting, developing and training our staff to motivate them.
  • Communicate its Quality Objectives and its performance against targeted objectives throughout the organization and to interested parties.
  • Train our staff for the appropriate implementation of Quality Management System and procedures in their work.
  • Work closely with our customers, suppliers and associate to establish the maximum Quality values.
  • Striving to continually improve our Quality Management System and our testing capabilities for Monitoring and Analysis to comply with highest standards.
  • Providing accurate and reliable results thus delivering a high level of customer satisfaction.
