Shesh Enviro Infra Pvt. Ltd. (SEIPL) is an Integrated waste management facility which entails all categories of treatment facilities under one roof.

Our Facilities

Common TSDF Facility Methodology

Hazardous Solid waste like Mix Filter Cake, ETP Sludge, Incineration Ash and Other Mixed Hazardous Waste shall be handled by SEIPL Common TSDF site and will be transported by road through dumpers/trucks.

Agitated Thin Film Dryer (ATFD)

Agitated thin-film dryer (ATFD) is used for continuous drying of heat-sensitive products under vacuum with total solvent recovery.

Common MEE Facility Methodology

MEE is a thermal treatment technology, where in the boiling point difference of different dissolved Solids, Chemicals, Organic, Inorganic Solvents are considered at different level of designing.

Stripper Column

Stripping columns are used to remove volatile hydrocarbons and volatile chlorinated hydrocarbons from water.
